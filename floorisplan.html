Who is this app for

FloorisPlan is built for those who work with measurements every day

Interior designers

Quickly create floor plans for design projects. All measurements in one place.

Foremen

Control room dimensions, utility placement, and work volumes on site.

Craftsmen

Record measurements, electrical and plumbing connection points. Everything at hand, right on your phone.

Features

A complete set of tools for professional measurement

Room creation

Draw rooms in top-down view. Set exact wall dimensions by voice.

Connecting through openings

Connect rooms through doorways. Link spaces into a unified plan.

Room repositioning

Freely move rooms on the plan. Build precise layouts.

Wall elevations

Automatic wall elevations for each room. See every wall in detail.

Electrical & plumbing points

Mark outlets, switches, water supply, sewage, and radiator points.

Complete inventory

Get a full measurement plan with elevations — a ready-to-use document.

Voice mode

Draw rooms by voice — dictate dimensions while your hands are busy with the tape measure.
